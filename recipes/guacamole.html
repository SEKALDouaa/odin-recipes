Guacamole

Description

This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro. You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes. Serve with homemade tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl
  2. Mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper
